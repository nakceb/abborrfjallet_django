Activities

Nature

Behind the cabin, you find the forrest that offers untouched nature to look for mushrooms or berries of various kind.

Accross the bay, you will find a nature reserved area with giant bowls from the inland ice era. Here, you will see clearly how the waterflow beneath the ice could create vortex causing a smaller stone carve out a big bowl in the rock.

Around the key, Nämndöfjärden widens out, surrounded by islands. A calm day, a short boat trip to Sandskär, an island next to the light house Franska stenarna (the French rocks) is highly recommended. There, on the sunny rocks on the south side, sunbath and brought picnic can be enjoyed while the pleasure boats passes by.

A tour by the canal to Stavsnäs/Vinterhamn is another appreciated ride. It is less dependent on calm weather as it is mainly in protected waters

The waters around Abborrfjället is well suited for sportfishing with rod from both cliffs and boat.

Near by activities

Golf

Only 7 km from Abborrfjället, you will find Fågelbro Golf & Country Club with a challanging 18-hole course. 13 km away, you will find the classic Wermdö Gold & Country Club with its lovely 18-hole course, and another 10 km away, Ingarö Golfklubb is welcoming you with their 18 hole forrest course and 18 hole park course.

Archipelago tour

From Stavsnäs Vinterhamn, you can take one of Waxholmsbolagets many boat tours to visit one of the popular islands like Sandhamn, Möja, Utö etc, or why not try Vaxholmsbolagets North/south line and discover the full coastline of Stockholm? For more information
