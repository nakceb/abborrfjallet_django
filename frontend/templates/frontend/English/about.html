To rent Abborrfjället

Abborrfjället is available for rent during the summer

We prefer renting it out via Airbnb for all parties convenience. Abborrfjället is rented weekly on a Saturday 3.00 pm to Saturday 11.00 am from the beginning of May to the end of October and is suitable for a family of 3-5 persons. Main cabin has in addition to the living room, a bedroom, kitchen and a bathroom with shower. The bedroom has a so-called family bed, which is a bunk bed with a wider 140 cm bed on the floor and a regular "80 cm bed" as a bunk bed.

There is also a comfortable bed sofa 140 cm width in the living room.

As Abborrfjället is a holiday home in our sensitive archipelago nature, the toilet issue is solved with a combustion toilet located in the former outhouse approx. 20 m from the main cabin. An undisturbed and picturesque location where the combustion smoke does not disturb the surroundings of the house. One way to combine the modern with the rural classic.

If additional beds are needed, the annex is available. A free standing small cabin that sits right next to the house, with the wooden deck tieing them together. There are two 90 cm single beds in the annexe.

Bed sheets and towels can be rented for SEK 150 per guest and stay if required.

The cottage should be cleand and returned in the same condition as when you arrived, and the used beds sheets should be placed in the laudry bag.

A small rowing boat is included, and for additional cost, a motor boat with an 18 hp motor could be disposed for fun water activities such as wake board for the youth, picnic tours etc.

History of Abborrfjället

Abborrfjäll was purchased in the early 30s by Folke and Elsie Stenbeck. Folke wanted a place in the archipelago to where he could sail, when sailing was his great passion.

The choice fell on Abborrfjället, as it is located in the heart of Stockholm's archipelago, as far out as can be reached on the mainland, with almost unlimited depth at the rocks to dock with his sailboat.

A small playhouse was erected at the top of the mountain where Folke and Elsie could spend the night when they visited Abborrfjället - yes, there were actually so big playhouses at that time. When the family began to grow in the mid-30's, the playhouse was replaced with a cabin where everyone was seated. Since then, Abborrfjället has been the natural hub for the family with its friends, and has hosted many social gatherings over the years. Since the house has been heavily serviced for a long time, it has undergone a renovation in the form of new cladding panel and new windows in 2018, and indoors has many surface layers have been renewed.

Location

Abborrfjället is located on Fågelbrolandet outside Värmdö, about 40 minutes by car from Stockholm city center.
